Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to MMUInsight's FAQ section. Find answers to common questions about registration, reviews, profiles, and more.

Registration & Account

What email should I use to register?

Use your MMU student email (@student.mmu.edu.my) for students or MMU staff email (@mmu.edu.my) for lecturers. Other emails won't be accepted.

Why do I need to verify my email?

Email verification prevents fake accounts and ensures only real MMU users can review.

Do I have to verify my email to use the platform?

Yes. You'll receive a verification link via email after signup. Click it to activate your account.

How long is the verification link valid?

24 hours. If it expires, use the "Resend verification email" link on the login page.

How do I delete my account?

Contact support. Account deletion will remove your reviews and associated data.

Can I change my email after registering?

Not currently. You'll need to create a new account with the new email.

Searching for Lecturers

How do I find a specific lecturer?

Use the search bar on the homepage. You can search by:

  • Lecturer's name (e.g., "Willie", "Aliah")
  • Email or email prefix (e.g., "tjchan", "klpoh")

The search uses fuzzy matching, so approximate spelling works too.

What do the stars and "New" label mean on search results?

"New" means the lecturer has no reviews yet. Stars show their average rating based on submitted reviews.

Reviews & Ratings

Can I write multiple reviews for the same lecturer?

No. You can only write ONE review per lecturer. However, you can edit your review anytime.

What do the 5 rating categories mean?

  • Clarity: How well they explain concepts
  • Engagement: How interesting/interactive their teaching is
  • Punctuality: How often they start/end on time
  • Responsiveness: How quickly they reply to questions
  • Fairness: How fair their grading/assessment is

How is the overall rating calculated?

Average of all 5 categories (rounded to 1 decimal place).

Can I edit or delete my review?

Yes. Go to the lecturer's profile, find your review, and click Edit or Delete.

What happens if I delete my review?

It's permanently removed. The lecturer's average rating will update accordingly.

Can I write an anonymous review?

Yes. Check "Submit anonymously" when writing. Only admins can see who wrote it.

What is the "Recommend" field?

It's a simple yes/no question: "Would you recommend this lecturer to other students?" This helps others quickly gauge overall satisfaction.

Anonymity & Privacy

If I write anonymously, will the lecturer know it's me?

No. The lecturer sees "Anonymous Student" instead of your name. Only admins can see your identity (for moderation purposes).

Can a lecturer see who reviewed them?

They can see all reviews and replies, but anonymous reviews display as "Anonymous Student".

What data do you collect from me?

Email, password (hashed), reviews you write, ratings you give, and replies you post. We don't share data with third parties.

Is my password secure?

Yes. Passwords are encrypted with bcrypt. We never store plain text passwords.

Lecturer Profiles

Can lecturers see their own profiles and reviews?

Yes. Lecturers can view their profiles and all reviews written about them. They can also reply to reviews and edit their bio.

Can a lecturer claim their profile?

Yes. Lecturers with official MMU email (@mmu.edu.my) can claim their profile during registration for verification.

Can lecturers reply to reviews?

Yes. Lecturers can write replies to any review about them. Replies are visible to all users.

Analytics

What analytics are available?

Both students and lecturers can view analytics showing:

  • Average ratings across all 5 categories
  • Overall rating (0-5 scale)
  • Total number of reviews
  • Rating distribution (how many ratings per score)
  • Comparison to other lecturers (students only)

Can I see the distribution of ratings?

Yes. The student analytics page shows how many reviews gave each rating (1-5) per category.

Suggestions & Voting

How do suggestions work?

Any user can suggest features or improvements. Others can upvote or downvote to show support. Admins use this feedback to prioritize new features.

Can I change my vote on a suggestion?

Yes. Click the same button again to remove your vote, or switch to the opposite vote.

Can I delete my suggestion?

You can't delete it yourself, but admins can remove inappropriate suggestions.

Bug Reports

How do I report a bug?

Click "Report Bug" in the navigation menu. Fill in the title and detailed description of the issue you encountered. Include steps to reproduce if possible.

Is my bug report visible to other users?

No. Bug reports are completely private and only visible to the admin team. We keep them confidential to prevent security vulnerabilities from being exploited.

How long does it take for admins to review my bug?

We aim to review all bug reports promptly. You can check the status (New, In Progress, Resolved, Closed) in your submitted report.

Can I see updates on my bug report?

Bug reports are private, so updates are managed internally by the admin team.

What counts as a bug?

Any technical issue such as broken features, error messages, performance problems, or unexpected behavior. For feature requests, use Suggestions instead.

Reports & Moderation

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the "Report" button under any review. Admins will investigate it within 24 hours.

What happens to reported reviews?

Admins investigate. If the content violates guidelines, it's removed and the user may be suspended.

Can admins delete reviews without a report?

Yes. Admins monitor content proactively to keep the platform safe.

What types of content are not allowed?

Content that is abusive, harassing, defamatory, sexually explicit, spam, or otherwise violates our Terms of Service.

Admin Features

What can admins do?

Admins can:

  • View and investigate bug reports
  • Pin important reviews to the top of lecturer profiles
  • Moderate content and handle user reports
  • View audit logs of all platform actions
  • Manage user roles and permissions

Can reviews be pinned?

Yes. Admins can pin reviews to the top of a lecturer's profile to highlight particularly helpful or important feedback.

Technical

What's the difference between dark mode and light mode?

Visual preference. Dark mode reduces eye strain; light mode is the standard view. Your preference is automatically saved.

What languages does MMUInsight support?

English, Malay, and Chinese. Change your language in the top menu.

Is MMUInsight mobile-friendly?

Yes, you can use it on phone, tablet, or desktop. The interface adapts to your screen size.

General

Why does MMUInsight exist?

To give students a transparent way to share feedback about teaching quality and help others choose lecturers wisely.

Is this affiliated with MMU officially?

No. This is a student-run project not officially endorsed by MMU administration. See our disclaimer on login/register pages.

How do I contact support?

Use the "Report Bug" feature to contact the admin team. For urgent issues, reach out through the contact information in the About Us page.
