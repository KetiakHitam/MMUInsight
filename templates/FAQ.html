Frequently Asked Questions

Registration & Account

What email should I use to register?

Use your MMU student email (@student.mmu.edu.my) for students or MMU staff email (@mmu.edu.my) for lecturers

Other emails won't be accepted


Why do I need to verify my email?

Email verification prevents fake accounts and ensures only real MMU users can review


Do I have to verify my email to use the platform?

Yes. You'll receive a verification link via email after signup. Click it to activate your account


How long is the verification link valid?

24 hours. If it expires, use the "Resend verification email" link on the login page


How do I delete my account?

Contact support. Account deletion will remove your reviews and associated data


Can I change my email after registering?

Not currently. You'll need to create a new account with the new email


Reviews & Ratings

Can I write multiple reviews for the same lecturer?

No. You can only write ONE review per lecturer, but you can edit it anytime


What do the 5 rating categories mean?

Clarity: How well they explain concepts

Engagement: How interesting/interactive their teaching is

Punctuality: How often they start/end on time

Responsiveness: How quickly they reply to questions

Fairness: How fair their grading/assessment is

How is the overall rating calculated?

Average of all 5 categories (rounded to 1 decimal place)


Can I edit or delete my review?

Yes. Go to the lecturer's profile, find your review, and click Edit or Delete


What happens if I delete my review?

It's permanently removed. The lecturer's average rating will update


Can I write an anonymous review?

Yes. Check "Submit anonymously" when writing. Only admins can see who wrote it


Anonymity & Privacy

If I write anonymously, will the lecturer know it's me?

No. The lecturer sees "Anonymous Student" instead of your name

Only admins can see your identity (for moderation)


Can a lecturer see who reviewed them?

They can see reviews and replies, but anonymous reviews show as "Anonymous Student"


What data do you collect from me?

Email, password (hashed), reviews you write, ratings you give, replies you post

We don't share data with third parties


Is my password secure?

Yes. Passwords are encrypted with bcrypt. We never store plain text passwords


Lecturer Profiles

How do I find a specific lecturer?

Use the search bar on the homepage and enter their email address


Why can't I see a lecturer's profile?

Lecturers only appear if they have at least one review. New lecturers won't show up until someone reviews them


Can lecturers see their own profiles and reviews?

Yes, but they can't edit/delete other people's reviews. They can only reply to reviews


Can a lecturer claim their profile?

Yes. Lecturers with official MMU email (@mmu.edu.my) can claim their profile for verification


Suggestions & Voting

How do suggestions work?

Any user can suggest features. Others upvote/downvote to show support

Admins use this feedback to prioritize new features


Can I change my vote on a suggestion?

Yes. Click the same button again to remove your vote, or switch to the opposite vote


Can I delete my suggestion?

You can't delete it yourself, but admins can remove inappropriate suggestions


Reports & Moderation

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the "Report" button under any review. Admins will review it within 24 hours


What happens to reported reviews?

Admins investigate. If the content violates guidelines, it's removed and the user may be suspended


Can admins delete reviews without a report?

Yes. Admins moderate content proactively to keep the platform safe


Technical

What's the difference between dark mode and light mode?

Visual preference. Dark mode reduces eye strain; light mode is standard. Your preference is saved


What languages does MMUInsight support?

English, Malay, and Chinese. Change your language in the top menu


Is MMUInsight mobile-friendly?

Yes, you can use it on phone, tablet, or desktop


What should I do if I encounter a bug?

Contact support with a description of the issue and screenshots if possible


General

Why does MMUInsight exist?

To give students a transparent way to share feedback about teaching quality and help others choose courses wisely


Is this affiliated with MMU officially?

No. This is a student-run project not officially endorsed by MMU administration
