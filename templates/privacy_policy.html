MMUInsight Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") sets out the basis on which MMUInsight ("we," "us," or "our") processes personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 ("PDPA") of Malaysia. This Policy describes how we collect, use, and process your Personal Data.

By using our website and services (the "Service"), you agree to the terms of this Policy. If you do not agree to this Policy, please do not use our Service.

1. What is Personal Data?

"Personal Data" refers to any information that can identify you as an individual, directly or indirectly, in a commercial transaction. This may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, and any other information you provide to us. We do not collect or process "sensitive personal data" (such as information about your health, political opinions, or religious beliefs) unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

2. Types of Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of Personal Data:

  • Identity Data: Your email address, username, and other identifying information you provide during account creation.
  • Account Data: Your user type (student, lecturer, or admin), password hash, bio, and profile information.
  • Content Data: Reviews, ratings, replies, and comments you submit on the platform.
  • Technical Data: Information about your use of the Service, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and a record of your activities on the site. This data is collected automatically through server logs and cookies.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Service, including the pages you visit and the features you use.

3. Purposes for Collecting and Processing Your Personal Data

We collect and process your Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service and to deliver its features to you.
  • To manage your account, including verification and profile claiming.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To generate and display lecturer reviews, ratings, and analytics.
  • To facilitate two-way communication between students and lecturers through reviews and replies.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service.
  • To monitor the usage of the Service and improve its functionality.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or violations of these Terms.
  • To enforce our rights and comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Collect Your Personal Data

We collect your Personal Data from the following sources:

  • Directly from you: When you register for an account, fill out forms on our website, submit reviews, or contact us.
  • Automatically: As you use our Service, through the use of cookies and other tracking technologies.

5. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We will not disclose your Personal Data to any third party without your prior consent, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • To our service providers and partners who assist us in operating the Service. These third parties are contractually obligated to process your data only as instructed by us and to maintain its confidentiality and security.
  • If required by law or in good faith to comply with a legal obligation, to protect and defend our rights or property, or to protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.
  • To platform administrators for the purposes of moderating content and managing the platform.

6. Security of Your Personal Data

We are committed to protecting the security of your Personal Data. We implement and maintain practical and reasonable security measures to protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your passwords are hashed using bcrypt for maximum security. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Retention of Personal Data

We will only retain your Personal Data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When you delete your account, your personal information will be removed, though anonymized review data may be retained for analytics purposes.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use session cookies to store your language preference and authentication information. These cookies are essential for the operation of the Service. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

9. Anonymous Reviews

You have the option to submit reviews anonymously. When you select this option, your name will not be displayed with your review. However, administrators may still have access to submission logs for moderation and security purposes.

10. Your Rights as a Data Subject

In accordance with the PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your Personal Data that we hold.
  • Request correction of your Personal Data if it is inaccurate, incomplete, misleading, or not up-to-date.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated Personal Data.
  • Withdraw your consent to the processing of your Personal Data for any purpose.

11. Consequences of Not Consenting to This Policy

The provision of your Personal Data is voluntary. However, if you choose not to provide us with the necessary Personal Data, we may not be able to provide you with the full functionality of our Service or create an account.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
