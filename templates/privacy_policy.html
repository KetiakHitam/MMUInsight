Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

MMUInsight is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the site includes:

  • Email Address: Required for account registration and communication
  • User Type: Whether you are a student, lecturer, or administrator
  • Reviews and Ratings: Content you submit about lecturers
  • Replies and Comments: Your interactions with reviews
  • Account Information: Any profile information you choose to provide
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the platform

3. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the site to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate and display lecturer reviews and analytics
  • Email you regarding your account or activities
  • Fulfill and send you requested materials and information
  • Improve our website and services
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud and other illegal activity

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If required by law or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third parties that perform services for us
  • Administrators: Platform administrators may access your information for moderation and management purposes

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use session cookies to store your language preference and authentication information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

7. Anonymous Reviews

You have the option to submit reviews anonymously. When you select this option, your name will not be displayed with your review, though administrators may still have access to submission logs.

8. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support channels available on the platform.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

MMUInsight reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting to the website. Your continued use of the site following the posting of revised Privacy Policy terms means that you accept and agree to the changes.
