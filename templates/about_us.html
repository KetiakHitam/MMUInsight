A student-driven platform for sharing honest, constructive lecturer feedback at Multimedia University.

Our mission

Help students make informed course decisions and encourage continuous improvement through transparent, respectful feedback.

What we offer

📊 Multi-factor ratings

Rate clarity, engagement, punctuality, responsiveness, and fairness.

🔍 Fast search

Find lecturers quickly and compare sentiment at a glance.

💬 Discussion threads

Replies keep feedback constructive and context-rich.

🔒 Anonymous option

Share honestly while still supporting moderation and accountability.

✅ Verified lecturers

Lecturers can claim profiles and add a short bio and context.

💡 Community-driven improvements

Use Suggestions to propose features and vote on what matters.

Our values

  • Transparency — honest feedback improves quality
  • Respect — critique behavior and teaching, not people
  • Fairness — multiple perspectives matter
  • Community — build a healthier academic environment
  • Accountability — moderation supports safe participation

How it works

  1. Create an account with your MMU email
  2. Search lecturers and explore profiles
  3. Read reviews and rating breakdowns
  4. Write a review based on your experience
  5. Engage in discussion threads when helpful

For lecturers

Lecturer participation is welcomed. Claiming your profile lets you add context and respond constructively.

  • Write a short bio and set expectations
  • Reply to reviews and clarify misconceptions
  • View analytics (where available) to spot trends

Contact & support

For feature requests or feedback, use the Suggestions page. For account issues, contact your platform administrators.
