About Us

What is MMUInsight?

MMUInsight is a lecturer review platform designed for Multimedia University (MMU) students. It provides a space where students can share honest, constructive feedback about their academic experiences with lecturers.

Our Mission

To empower students with a voice in their educational journey by enabling them to share feedback with lecturers, administrators, and future students. We believe transparent, respectful feedback helps improve teaching quality and student satisfaction.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Ratings: Students rate lecturers on clarity, engagement, punctuality, responsiveness, and fairness
  • Anonymous Reviews: Option to submit reviews anonymously for honest feedback
  • Lecturer Verification: Lecturers can claim and verify their profiles using official MMU email addresses
  • Two-Way Dialogue: Lecturers can reply to reviews and students can engage in constructive discussion
  • Analytics Dashboard: Lecturers and administrators can view detailed analytics about feedback
  • Multi-Language Support: Available in English, Malay, and Chinese

User Types

  • Students: Can write reviews, reply to reviews, and report inappropriate content
  • Lecturers: Can view feedback, claim profiles, reply to reviews, and access analytics
  • Administrators: Can manage users, moderate content, and view platform-wide analytics

Community Guidelines

We are committed to maintaining a respectful and constructive community. All users are expected to:

  • Provide honest, constructive feedback based on personal experience
  • Treat others with respect and courtesy
  • Avoid posting personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory content
  • Not share confidential or sensitive information
  • Report any inappropriate content or behavior to administrators

Feedback and Support

We welcome your feedback and suggestions for improving MMUInsight. If you have questions, encounter issues, or would like to report inappropriate content, please contact the platform administrators.

Privacy and Security

Your privacy is important to us. Please review our Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.
